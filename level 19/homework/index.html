Audio Example

Explanation: - controls adds play, pause, and volume controls to the audio player. - autoplay automatically starts the audio when the page loads. - loop makes the audio repeat indefinitely. - preload="auto" ensures the audio is fully loaded when the page loads.

Video Example

Explanation: - controls adds play, pause, volume, and fullscreen controls to the video player. - autoplay starts playing the video automatically when the page loads. - loop makes the video play in a continuous loop. - muted makes the video silent when played. - preload="metadata" loads only the metadata (e.g., duration) before the video starts playing. - poster specifies an image (thumbnail.jpg) to be shown before the video starts playing. - width="320" height="240" sets the width and height of the video player.
